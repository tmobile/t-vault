    • Config item = NCLM
    • Description - Provide the details of the certificate
  2. Wait for PIER ticket approval
When approval is completed please follow the following steps to manage your certificate:
  1. Login to T-Vault using your CORP AD account.
  2. Navigate to the "MANAGE/ADMIN" tab.
  3. Select the "CERTIFICATE MANAGEMENT tab.
  4. Select the "View/Edit" option corresponding to the certificate you requested to view and manage requested certificate.
Your certificate can be downloaded from T-Vault by going here : T-Vault.
  1. Navigate to the "CERTIFICATES" tab.
  2. Search for requested certificate.
  3. Select the certificate to download.
